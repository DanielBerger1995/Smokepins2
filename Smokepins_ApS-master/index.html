-  What are Smokepins?  -

Hi, I am Ole Gravrok a passionate fly fisherman, always on the search for new and delicious food. With Smokepins we want to share a convenient method, that allows you to enjoy a unique taste and experience in cold smoking your own food. This new invention makes cold smoking simple, transportable and quick.

Read more

-  Why Smokepins?  -

Hand-made

Each Smokepin is hand-made with love and dedication.

Transportable

Easy to store at home and take outdoors, it fits in your pocket!

Convenient

Cold smoking is now much faster than any traditional method.

Authentic taste

Find your own unique taste of cold smoked food and enjoy the flavours.
